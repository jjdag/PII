Chef Antoine's tastiest Namoura

Ingredients:

  • 500g fine semolina
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 spoons ghee
  • 5 spoons powdered milk
  • 1 and 1/2 spoons yeast
  • 1/2 spoon sodium bicarbonate
  • 2 spoons tahini
  • 30g almonds

.

.

.

  • 750g sugar
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 1 spoon orange blossom water

Steps

  1. First combine the semolina, flour, sugar, and ghee until homogenous.
  2. Dilute the milk in some warm water and add the yeast into it to activate it.
  3. Add the milk mixture into the semolina mix, along with the sodium bicarbonate.
  4. Brush some tahini into your baking pan and evenly spread the thickened semolina batter into it.
  5. Cut the batter into diamond sections with a butter knife then bake at 150 degrees C until golden.
  6. With your remaining sugar, lemon zest, and orange blossom water, make a syrup over medium heat.
  7. After removing the namoura from the oven, pour your syrup over it while still warm. Let cool and enjoy!
